Why QRTagAll

Everything you need to tag your world

From a lost wallet to a factory floor, QRTagAll gives every object a secure, scannable identity — without any app to install.

Universal Tagging

Attach a smart QR identity to any physical object — tools, plants, ID cards, furniture, vehicles, or documents. If it exists, you can tag it.

Zero App Required

How It Works

Tag anything in three steps

Your QR is cryptographically generated, universally scannable, and fully yours — ready in seconds, no account needed to start.

1

Generate Your QR

Click "Generate QR" to instantly create a unique, cryptographically signed QR code. Each ID is timestamp-encoded and globally unique.

2

Print & Attach

Print or save your QR code. Stick it on anything — a tool, plant pot, wallet, machine, document, or ID card.

3

Scan & Discover

Anyone who scans sees exactly what you've chosen to share. Update it anytime, from anywhere — no special app required to scan.
