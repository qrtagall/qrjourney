Why QRTagAll

Universal Tagging

Attach a smart QR identity to any physical object — tools, plants, ID cards, furniture, vehicles, or documents. If it exists, you can tag it.

How It Works

Tag anything in three steps

Your QR is cryptographically generated, universally scannable, and fully yours — ready in seconds, no account needed to start.

1

Generate Your QR

Click "Generate QR" to instantly create a unique, cryptographically signed QR code. Each ID is timestamp-encoded and globally unique.

2

Print & Attach

Print or save your QR code. Stick it on anything — a tool, plant pot, wallet, machine, document, or ID card.

3

Scan & Discover

Anyone who scans sees exactly what you've chosen to share. Update it anytime, from anywhere — no special app required to scan.

About QRTagAll

The universal identity layer for the physical world

QRTagAll was built on a simple belief: every object deserves a digital voice. From the plant on your windowsill to a machine on a factory floor, the physical world is full of things that carry stories, histories, and critical information — but no way to share them.

We built QRTagAll to change that. A universal, open tagging system where anyone can generate a secure QR identity, attach it to anything, and decide exactly what the world sees when they scan it. Scalable from one person to an entire enterprise.

Scalable. Privacy-respecting. No centralized database required.
